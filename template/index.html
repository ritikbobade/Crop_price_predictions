About us

We at Agrometer, help you to predict the crop's prices, which can be helpful to you to decide your future investments in particular crop.

We predict the future crop prices so it's helpful for agricultural, food as well as fertilzer sectors to plan accordingly.

We predict the crop's prices on the basis of three factors, so it's helpful for farmers from every region.

our services

Geographical Predictions

Prediction on the basis of geography will help farmers from various part of the state to decide their future crop. Geographical predictions include various districts and predict the feasible crops can be grown there.

Climatic Predictions

Predictions on the basis of climate will help the farmers from the regions where climate is the main concern and help them select their future crop. Climatic predictions include factors like air humidity, temperature and rainfall to predict the crop's price.

Predictions on previous datasets

Predictions on the basis of previous datasets include the datasets of past to predict the future crop price.
